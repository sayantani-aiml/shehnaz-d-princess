Our Story

The Princess
Manifesto

Philosophy

Where Every Woman Feels Like Royalty

Origin

Located in the heart of Shibpur, Howrah, Shahnaz D Princess was founded on a simple philosophy: every woman deserves to feel like royalty. Not just on her wedding day, not just on special occasions — but every single time she walks through our doors.

Sanctuary

As a proudly women-owned enterprise, we have cultivated a safe, welcoming, and LGBTQ+ friendly sanctuary where you can escape the rush of daily life. From the ambient lighting to the soothing music, every detail is designed to calm your mind while we pamper your body.

Artistry

We don't believe in "one size fits all." Our team of highly trained artisans combines the latest techniques with time-tested beauty wisdom to deliver results that speak for themselves. Every hair spa, facial, and styling session is tailored specifically to your unique features and desires.

Trust

With a 4.8★ rating and over 811 genuine reviews, we are deeply grateful for the unwavering trust our community has placed in us. Customer satisfaction isn't just a goal; it is our standard. And it's a standard we renew with every single client we serve.
